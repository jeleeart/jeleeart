Commissions

Commissions are currently open!




By commissioning me you agree to my Terms of Service.

How it Works:

♡ Click the button above to send a request and begin the process.
♡ I will contact you via email to verify your request and give you my price.
♡ I will not start the commission until I’ve received payment.
♡ After receiving payment I will begin sketching.
♡ I will send you a progress picture when I’ve completed the sketch.
♡ At this phase you can ask for any revisions or changes.
♡ Typically the turnaround time is 2 weeks.
♡ I will send completed digital artwork via email.

Pricing:

♡ Payments must be made upfront.
♡ I will only accept payments through Paypal or Stripe.
♡ Pricing varies depending on the complexity of the commission. I do my best to estimate baseline prices but I will only be able to give you a definite price after I know the details of your commission.
♡ Pricing for commercial use is double to triple the price of personal commissions depending on the type of commercial use. (ie selling prints, merch, book covers, etc)

Will Draw:

♡ Original Characters
♡ Dungeons & Dragons Characters
♡ Furry/Anthro
♡ Gore/Blood
♡ NSFW

Won’t Draw:

♡ Lolicon
♡ Certain ships/fandoms (feel free to ask)
♡ Anything offensive (racism, transphobia, homophobia)
